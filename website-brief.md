# Synapse D2C Website Brief (India)

## 1) One-page website structure (wireframe)

> **Assumptions:** Single SKU chewing gum with natural caffeine. India market focus. Legal language avoids medical claims.

1. **Hero**
   - **Purpose:** Instant product clarity + primary conversion CTA.
   - **Headline options:**
     1. “Clean focus in every chew.”
     2. “Chew your way to clearer, calmer focus.”
     3. “Functional gum for modern focus.”
   - **Supporting copy (1–2 sentences):** “Synapse is a clean-label, focus-supporting gum with natural caffeine and calming botanicals—no jitters, no crash. Start your day sharper in seconds.”
   - **CTA:** “Buy Synapse” / “Try it today”
   - **UX elements:** Primary CTA button + secondary “Learn how it works” anchor; trust microcopy (“Ships across India • 30-day happiness guarantee”); price/servings badge.
   - **A/B test priority:** Headline framing (performance vs. calm focus), CTA text, trust badges (lab-tested vs. clean-label).
   - **Mobile layout:** Hero stack—product image, headline, short copy, CTA, price badge.
   - **AMP-lite:** Title, 1-sentence value prop, price, CTA, 1 trust line.

2. **Benefits (3-up)**
   - **Purpose:** Communicate key outcomes quickly.
   - **Headline options:**
     1. “Focus you can feel.”
     2. “Why Synapse works for busy days.”
     3. “What you get in every chew.”
   - **Supporting copy:** “A fast, clean alternative to coffee—designed to support alertness and steady focus.”
   - **CTA:** “See ingredients”
   - **UX elements:** Icons + short benefit bullets.
   - **A/B test priority:** Benefit order; “energy” vs. “focus” language.
   - **Mobile layout:** Carousel cards.
   - **AMP-lite:** 3 bullets.

3. **How it works**
   - **Purpose:** Explain mechanism without medical claims.
   - **Headline options:**
     1. “How Synapse works”
     2. “Fast focus, explained”
     3. “Chew → absorb → focus support”
   - **Supporting copy:** “Chewing delivers ingredients quickly and steadily to support alertness and mental clarity.”
   - **CTA:** “Learn more”
   - **UX elements:** 3-step diagram; source links placeholder.
   - **A/B test priority:** Step visuals vs. short paragraph.
   - **Mobile layout:** Stacked steps.
   - **AMP-lite:** 3-step list.

4. **Ingredients & clean label**
   - **Purpose:** Build trust and transparency.
   - **Headline options:**
     1. “Clean ingredients, clearly listed.”
     2. “What’s inside Synapse.”
     3. “Simple, functional formula.”
   - **Supporting copy:** “Natural caffeine and functional botanicals, with no artificial colors.”
   - **CTA:** “View full label”
   - **UX elements:** Ingredient cards + “why we chose it.”
   - **A/B test priority:** Ingredient order + graphic vs. table.
   - **Mobile layout:** Accordion by ingredient.
   - **AMP-lite:** Ingredient list + 1-line summary.

5. **Social proof**
   - **Purpose:** Reduce risk with reviews and UGC.
   - **Headline options:**
     1. “Trusted by busy minds.”
     2. “Real reviews from real routines.”
     3. “Focus, without the fuss.”
   - **Supporting copy:** “Short, verified reviews focused on taste and focus support.”
   - **CTA:** “Read reviews”
   - **UX elements:** Star rating summary + 3 review tiles.
   - **A/B test priority:** Review source (UGC vs. verified purchase).
   - **Mobile layout:** Swipeable review cards.
   - **AMP-lite:** 2 short reviews + rating.

6. **Subscription & savings**

7. **FAQ**

## 2) Home page visual and content guidelines

- **Visual style:** Minimal, modern, science-forward, plenty of white space.
- **Typography:**
  - Headings: Grotesk/Sans (e.g., “Inter”, “Söhne”, “Manrope”).
  - Body: Clean sans (e.g., “Inter”, “Work Sans”).
  - Avoid decorative fonts.
- **Imagery guidance:** Product-forward with clean backgrounds; lifestyle shots for professionals/students; macro texture of gum.
- **Iconography:** Thin-line, consistent stroke; use for benefits and ingredients.

**Moodboard keywords**
- “clinical clean,” “focused minimalism,” “bright neutrals,” “calm energy,” “precise typography.”

**Color palettes (hex)**
- **Palette A (clean tech):** #0B0F14 (ink), #F6F7F9 (off-white), #5CC8D7 (aqua), #F5C542 (warm accent), #B5BCC6 (neutral).
- **Palette B (calm focus):** #101820 (charcoal), #FFFFFF (white), #6E8E7E (sage), #FFB38A (soft coral), #D9DEE5 (mist).

**Photography brief**
- **Hero shot:** Single pack on clean background + gum piece; high-key lighting. Copy direction: “instant clarity, clean label.”
- **Lifestyle:** Office desk, student study, creative studio; focus on calm energy. Copy direction: “supports focus without harsh spikes.”
- **Pack shots:** Front, back (nutrition/ingredients), open pack with gums. Copy direction: “transparent ingredients.”

**Microcopy examples**
- Cart: “Free shipping over ₹X • Ships in 24–48 hours.”
- Shipping: “Estimated delivery: 2–5 business days.”
- Error: “Please check your address—this looks incomplete.”
- Checkout: “Secure checkout • PCI compliant.”

## 4) Checkout & conversion flow

**Recommended steps**
1. Cart (one click to checkout; show savings).
2. Checkout (guest by default; express pay).
3. Confirmation (cross-sell + subscription prompt).

**Friction-minimizing techniques**
- Guest checkout, autofill, PIN-code lookup, address validation.
- Express pay: Apple Pay/Google Pay/UPI.
- Progress indicator + inline error validation.

**Upsell/cross-sell & subscription UX**
- One-click subscription toggle with savings badge.
- Trial pack add-on (if available) or 2-pack upsell.
- “Subscribe & save” pre-selected with clear opt-out.

**Compliance checklist**
- **GDPR:** cookie consent, data access/deletion requests.
- **COPPA:** avoid targeting under-13; no child data capture.
- **PCI:** use compliant payment provider; no card data stored.

## 7) Technical requirements & deployment checklist

**Stack options**
- **Static JAMstack:** Fast, low cost; needs headless commerce integration.
- **Headless Shopify:** Flexible UI, scalable; more dev complexity.
- **Shopify + custom frontend:** Faster to launch, solid ecosystem; less flexibility.
- **Shopify Hydrogen:** Performance-focused; higher build complexity.

**Accessibility checklist (WCAG AA)**
- Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1
- Keyboard navigation
- Alt text for imagery
- Semantic headings

**Performance checklist**
- Optimize images, lazy load
- Minify CSS/JS
- Use CDN caching
- Limit third-party scripts

**Security/SEO checklist**
- HTTPS everywhere
- CSP and secure headers
- Unique meta tags, schema.org product data
- Clean URLs + sitemap

**CI/CD & hosting**
- CI: GitHub Actions
- Hosting: Vercel/Netlify for headless; Shopify for standard

**Rollout plan**
- Staging → soft launch to email list → full launch + paid ads.
